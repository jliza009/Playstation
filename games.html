GTA V

"Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) by Rockstar Games is an action-packed adventure set in the sprawling city of Los Santos. Players step into the lives of three diverse criminals, experiencing a gripping narrative filled with heists, betrayal, and relentless pursuit

Red Dead Redemption II

"Red Dead Redemption II (RDR2) is an epic western adventure by Rockstar Games, immersing players in the American Wild West. Following the story of outlaw Arthur Morgan in the Van der Linde gang, the game explores a vast open-world filled with moral choices and dynamic landscapes

Fortnite

"Fortnite, developed by Epic Games, is a cultural gaming phenomenon that blends fast-paced action with creative gameplay. Set on an ever-evolving island, players engage in Battle Royale mode, competing to be the last one standing.

PlayStation is a series of gaming consoles created and developed by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The brand was first introduced in December 1994 with the launch of the original PlayStation console.
